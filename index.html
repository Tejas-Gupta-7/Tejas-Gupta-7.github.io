The Numismatic Gallery {Estd. 2020}


Numismatic Gallery is an interface to facilitate the posting and fetching of information of currency which is part of our collection. The idea is to develop a program that will allow viewers of the collection to look for the currency they are interested in by entering the country it belongs to. In case the collector does not have any currency of that country, it will allow the user to add information about it from his side by filling details in fields like year, description, country, denomination and composition.
